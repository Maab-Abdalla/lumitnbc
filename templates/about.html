About

Clarity for the TNBC journey

LumiTNBC is a research-driven decision-support tool that classifies Triple-Negative Breast Cancer into its molecular subtypes and explains each result in language patients and clinicians can both follow.

Who it's for

Patients

Understand your subtype in plain language and see what shaped the result.

Providers

Review classifications with feature-level detail and send feedback to patients.

How it works

1

Input your data

Provide gene expression data, clinical and pathology information, or a combination of both.

2

Model prediction

A gradient-boosted model (XGBoost) predicts the most likely subtype — BL1, BL2, LAR, or M.

3

Explainable result

SHAP attributes the prediction to specific features, so the result is never a black box.

4

Human in the loop

Patients can request a written review from a healthcare provider before acting on anything.
